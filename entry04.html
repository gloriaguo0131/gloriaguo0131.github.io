When i recall banff national park I remember the remote canadian wlderness. Majestic forests of pines, falling onto my shoulder. The abundance of animal wildlife, Longhorn sheep, moose walking by our car. I saw the stars last night sprawled across the sky. City girl has never felt so big, yet so small. It was there that i felt like something And also like nothing at all.
